# REMI PARK AND THE GARAGE OF DOOM
## By Bex Castillo

*For every kid who has ever looked at something and thought, "I bet I could build that better."*

*You probably can.*

---

## About the Author

Bex Castillo grew up in San Jose, California, where she spent most of her childhood taking apart appliances her parents were still using. The daughter of a Korean American mother (née Park) and a Filipino father, she learned to fix things by watching her lola and her mom handle every household repair themselves. She studied mechanical engineering at Cal Poly before realizing she'd rather write about kids who build things than sit in meetings about load-bearing specifications. She has worked as a science museum educator, a robotics camp counselor, and briefly as a roller coaster inspector, which was exactly as fun as it sounds. Remi's surname is a nod to her mom's side of the family, and the character's relentless tinkering is drawn pretty directly from the women who raised her. Bex lives in Oakland with her partner, two kids, and a garage that the neighbors regard with suspicion. *Remi Park and the Garage of Doom* is her first novel.
